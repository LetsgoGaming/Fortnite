Fortnite

Fortnite is one of the most popular games on the web, being made for younger children also being played by adults. Fortnite found its begigning in 2017, when epic games released Fortnite it was all the craze and everyone was going crazy about it and trying it themselves. Fortnite rose top popularity very quickly and exploding all over social media platforms, some streamers streaming fortnite would average around 100,000 viewers every stream! Fortnite is a fun to play multiplayer game with many diffrent gamemode to choose from including public matches and competitve arena. Fortnite also always hosts tournaments, there are two types of tournaments, one is a open league where anyone can compete. And then there is a champion league tournament where you have to be in champs to compete, this is what most professinal players play. Fortnite's competitve scene is also very lively as the prize pools of many tournaments can be huge, the fortnite world cup itself was over 3 million usd for first place and in total maybe even over 10 million usd for the entire tournament. Fortnite also can host creator related tournaments, recently fortnite released the bugha cup which had a total prize pool of 100,000 usd!
